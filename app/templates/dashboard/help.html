Common Questions

How do I edit a transaction?

Go to the Transactions page, find the transaction you want to edit, and click the edit icon. Make your changes and save.

Can I delete accounts that have transactions or transfers?

No, accounts with associated transactions or transfers cannot be deleted. You must first remove or reassign all transactions and transfers before deleting an account.

Can I delete categories that have transactions?

You can only delete categories that don't have any associated transactions. Remove or reassign transactions first.

How is my balance calculated?

Your balance starts with your initial amount and is updated by adding income transactions and subtracting expense transactions.

Can I backup, restore, or permanently delete my data?

Yes, you can save your data for backup purposes, restore from previous backups, or permanently delete all your data and account. These options are available in your Profile settings for complete data management control.

Is my data secure?

Yes, your data is protected with industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections and secure authentication.
